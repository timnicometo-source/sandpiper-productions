The Heritage in the Name

Before it was a media production company, “The Sandpiper” was the name of my grandmother’s personal art studio—a place of inspiration and craft that spanned the critical art scenes of the 1970s and ’80s.

The phrase "grandmother's art" often evokes images of quaint, traditional oil paintings, but she completely defied that stereotype. She was a highly talented rebel and a forward-thinking creator who operated well ahead of her time. Sandpiper Productions carries that specific, boundary-pushing legacy into the digital age.

Just as she challenged the artistic norms of her era, we apply that same unconventional energy to our technical projects today. We aren’t simply building websites; we are engineering digital art that is a direct, modern descendant of her original creative haven.

Sandpiper Productions is a tribute to a creative spirit that believed in making something meaningful and lasting.

— In honor of my grandmother
